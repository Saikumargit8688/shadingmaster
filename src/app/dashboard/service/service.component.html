CHILD SAFETY

On average, one child dies each year in Canada from strangulation
caused by corded window coverings. This number represents a tragic loss
that could have been prevented.

Recognizing the urgency of the issue, Health Canada has
implemented substantial changes to its regulations governing the
manufacturing and sale of window coverings in the country. These revisions
are a step in the right direction and are welcomed by advocacy groups such
as Parents for Window Blind Safety, who have been tirelessly campaigning
for stricter regulations in the industry for the past 12 years.
